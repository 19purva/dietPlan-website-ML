BREAKFAST

Details for Moong dal chilla


  • Moong dal (yellow split gram)
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt
  • Oil

Recipe Instructions:


  • Soak moong dal in water for 2 hours.
  • Drain and grind to a smooth batter with green chilies, ginger, and cumin seeds.
  • Add turmeric powder and salt.
  • Heat oil in a pan, pour a ladleful of batter, and spread it into a thin circle.
  • Cook until golden brown on both sides.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cook Time: 5-7 minutes per chilla.
  • Preparation Time: 2 hours (soaking) + 10 minutes (batter preparation)
  • Total Time: Approximately 30 minutes

Details for Smoothie bowl


  • 1 banana (frozen)
  • 1/2 cup berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Toppings: granola, fresh fruit, nuts, seeds

Recipe Instructions:


  • In a blender, combine the frozen banana, berries, Greek yogurt, and almond milk.
  • Blend until smooth. Adjust consistency with more almond milk if needed.
  • Pour into a bowl and top with granola, fresh fruit, nuts, and seeds.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cooking Time: None
  • Preparation Time: 5-10 minutes
  • Total Time: 5-10 minutes

Details for Pesarattu (Green Gram Dosa)


  • 1 cup green gram (moong beans)
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for batter consistency)
  • Oil for cooking

Recipe Instructions:


  • Soak green gram overnight or for at least 6 hours.
  • Drain and grind with green chilies, ginger, and cumin seeds to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  • Add salt to taste.
  • Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladleful of batter, and spread it thin like a dosa.
  • Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cooking Time: 5-7 minutes per dosa
  • Preparation Time: 6 hours (soaking) + 10 minutes (batter preparation)
  • Total Time: Approximately 6 hours 30 minutes

Details for Vegetable Oats


  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped beans
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • Water (2 cups)

Recipe Instructions:


  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté until they crackle.
  • Add chopped onions and green chili, and sauté until onions turn golden.
  • Add the chopped vegetables and turmeric powder, cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Pour in water and bring it to a boil.
  • Add oats and salt, cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Cook until the oats absorb the water and the vegetables are tender.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cooking Time: 10-12 minutes
  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20-25 minutes

Details for Soya Chunks Curry


  • 1 cup soya chunks
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Recipe Instructions:


  • Boil soya chunks in water for 5-7 minutes. Drain and rinse under cold water. Squeeze out excess water.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and green chilies, sauté until onions turn golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Add the boiled soya chunks and garam masala, mix well. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25-30 minutes

Details for Vegetable Dal Khichdi


  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe Instructions:


  • Rinse rice and dal together. Set aside.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Add the mixed vegetables, rice, and dal, and mix well.
  • Add water (about 3-4 cups) and pressure cook for 3-4 whistles.
  • Let the pressure release naturally, then open the cooker and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25-30 minutes

Details for Quinoa upma


  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe Instructions:


  • Rinse the quinoa under cold water. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and set aside.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add onions and green chili. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  • Add mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes until they are tender.
  • Add the cooked quinoa and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cook Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: Approximately 25-30 minutes

Details for Grilled Paneer


  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1 tablespoon yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe Instructions:


  • In a bowl, mix yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, garam masala, lemon juice, oil, and salt.
  • Add paneer cubes to the mixture and marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  • Preheat a grill pan or tawa and lightly grease with oil.
  • Grill the marinated paneer cubes on each side until golden and crisp.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cook Time: 10-12 minutes
  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes + 20 minutes marination
  • Total Time: 30-35 minutes

Details for Vegetable Stew


  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, potatoes, beans, peas)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh curry leaves for garnish

Recipe Instructions:


  • Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  • Add onions, green chilies, and ginger, sauté until onions are translucent.
  • Add turmeric powder and mixed vegetables, sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add water and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  • Stir in coconut milk and simmer for 5 minutes. Add salt to taste.
  • Garnish with fresh curry leaves before serving.

Cooking and Preparation Time:


  • Cook Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25-30 minutes
